The Stuntman

The Stuntman is an action-comedy film directed by David Leitch (Bullet Train, Deadpool 2) based on the hit 1980s series "Hard at Fall." The story follows Colt Seavers (Ryan Gosling), a Hollywood stuntman who had to abandon his dangerous stunt career after suffering an accident that nearly ended his life and career.

Deadpool & Wolverine

"Deadpool and Wolverine (2024)" is the typical fan-service movie, which might be less impactful for those who have little appreciation for those characters. Unfortunately, I was one of them. I enjoyed the soundtrack (which humorously mocks what one expects to hear in a superhero film), some action scenes, and the quips that Deadpool made.
