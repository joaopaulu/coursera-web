About Fender

The History of Fender

Fender Musical Instruments Corporation was founded in 1946 by Leo Fender, a visionary who changed the music world with his innovations. Since then, Fender basses have been favorites among musicians of all genres.

The Fender Precision Bass, released in 1951, was the first solid-body electric bass in history, revolutionizing the way the instrument was played. The Jazz Bass, created in 1960, brought more versatility with its design and bright sound.
